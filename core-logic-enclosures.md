# Core Logic Enclosures

## III. THE MASTER ARCHITECTURE DIRECTORY (COMPACT BLUEPRINT v4.0)

This section maps the active foundational wrappers handling structural pitch manipulation, harmony generation, duet dynamics, and deep character matrix isolation using the optimized `#Category.Value#` protocol.

### 1. The Text-Case Vocal Contour Grid (Pitch & Frequency)
* `#Pw#` (Whistle Register): Bypasses normal hardware constraints to generate ultra-high, soaring, or piercing vocal peaks.
* `#Pu#` (Up One Register): Forces the target vocal track up exactly one full register class.
* `#Pn#` (Normalized): Resets the pitch to the standard vocal register.
* `#Pd#` (Down One Register): Forces the target vocal track down exactly one full register class.
* `#Ps#` (Sub-Harmonic Rumble): Drops the audio delivery into the absolute lowest floor of the audible sound spectrum for deep, floor-rattling bass resonance.

### 2. The Backing Vocal & Harmony Deck (#BV.#)
* `#Bm#` / `#Bf#`: Threads background Male or Female studio harmonies directly under the lead track.
* `#Bc#` / `#Bm_c#` / `#Bf_c#`: Threads a generic, male, or female backing choir directly behind the lead performance.

### 3. The Duet Choreography & Punctuation Framework (#()K+/-#)
* `#()K+#` (Continuity Link): Instructs the parser that the current singer’s phrase is an open fragment, keeping their vocal channel live while weaving the alternative track beneath it.
* `#K-#` (Anchor Stop): Acts as a hard structural ceiling that closes the segment and clears the tracking slate.

### 4. The Encapsulated Vocal Age & Character Matrix (#M.#)
Ensures absolute separation of character traits from standard lyrics. Fully stackable.
* `#Mch#`: Child Age Register Profile.
* `#My#`: Young Adult Register Profile.
* `#Ma#`: Adult Register Profile.
* `#Mo#`: Old Adult Register Profile.
* `#Maos#`: Ancient Soul Register Profile.
* `#M_c#`: The Ensemble/Choir Anchor.
* `#Mm#` / `#Mf#`: Male / Female Gender Voice Matrix.
* `#Mt#`: Together (Duet Casting Identifier).
