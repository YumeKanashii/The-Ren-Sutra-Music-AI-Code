# Core Logic Enclosures
## III. THE MASTER ARCHITECTURE DIRECTORY (COMPACT BLUEPRINT v3.0)

This section maps the active foundational wrappers handling structural pitch manipulation, harmony generation, duet dynamics, and deep character matrix isolation.

### 1. The Text-Case Vocal Contour Grid (Pitch & Frequency)
* +TEXT+ / +text+ (Single Plus Container): Forces the target vocal track up exactly one full register class (e.g., Alto to Soprano).
* -TEXT- / -text- (Single Minus Container): Forces the target vocal track down exactly one full register class (e.g., Tenor to Bass).
* ++TEXT++ (Double Plus Container): The Whistle Register Protocol. Bypasses normal hardware constraints to generate ultra-high, soaring, or piercing vocal peaks.
* --text-- (Double Minus Container): The Sub-Harmonic Rumble Protocol. Drops the audio delivery into the absolute lowest floor of the audible sound spectrum for deep, floor-rattling bass resonance.

### 2. The Backing Vocal & Harmony Deck ({...})
* {m} / {f}: Threads background Male or Female studio harmonies directly under the lead track.
* {c} / {cm} / {cf}: Threads a generic, male, or female backing choir directly behind the lead performance.

### 3. The Duet Choreography & Punctuation Framework
* ; (Semicolon): The Continuity Link. Instructs the parser that the current singer’s phrase is an open fragment, keeping their vocal channel live while seamlessly weaving the alternative duet track beneath it.
* . (Period): The Anchor Stop. Acts as a hard structural ceiling that closes the duet segment and clears the tracking slate.

### 4. The Encapsulated Vocal Age & Character Matrix (^...^)
Ensures absolute separation of character traits from standard lyrics. Fully stackable.
* ch: Child Age Register Profile.
* y: Young Adult Register Profile (Systemically isolated to prevent 'Together' syntax errors).
* a: Adult Register Profile.
* o: Old Adult Register Profile.
* _C: The Ensemble/Choir Anchor. Programmatically flags a multi-singer choir formation.
* m / f: Male / Female Gender Voice Matrix.
* T: Together Duet Casting Identifier.
* Verification Example: ^ch_Cm^ = Encapsulated Male Child Choir Solo.
* Verification Example: ^^o_Cm^ = Encapsulated Ancient Male Choir (Utilizing the double-caret ^^o Older Soul protocol).
